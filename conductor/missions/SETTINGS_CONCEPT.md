# Settings Page — Full App Configuration

**Created**: 2026-04-01
**Type**: Concept / Integration Target

---

## 9 Sections

1. **Appearance** — 10 themes with live mini-previews, palette display, font size/density/sidebar position, editor toggles
2. **Profile** — Avatar, name, email, website, bio
3. **Business** — Business name, tax ID, hourly/day rates, minimum project, rush multiplier
4. **Brand & Portal** — Client portal customization, headline, accent color picker, custom domain
5. **Notifications** — Email/push/digest delivery + event toggles (proposals, payments, overdue, comments, Wire)
6. **Integrations** — Stripe, Google Calendar, Gmail, Zoom, Slack, Figma, Notion, Drive
7. **Plan & Billing** — Free vs Pro, revenue summary with fee breakdown
8. **Security** — Password, 2FA toggle, active sessions, delete account
9. **Data & Export** — Export (projects, financial, clients, full backup), import (HoneyBook, Notion, CSV), retention settings

## Prototype Reference

Full standalone prototype with all 9 sections, theme-aware rendering, and 10 theme mini-preview cards.
