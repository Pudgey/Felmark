# Mission: Theme System — 5 Themes, Terminal-Controlled

**Created**: 2026-03-31
**Status**: Planning
**Milestone**: M1

---

## Goal

A freelancer types `/theme` in the terminal, sees live mini-previews of all 5 themes (Ember, Midnight, Sage, Clay, Frost), selects one, and the entire app transforms in real time — every background, border, text color, and accent shifts instantly via CSS variable swap.

---

## The 5 Themes

| Theme | Vibe | Accent | Inspiration |
|-------|------|--------|-------------|
| **Ember** | Warm parchment, golden accents | #b07d4f | Game of Thrones stone halls, aged paper, forge fire |
| **Midnight** | Deep navy, electric blue | #4a9eff | Bloomberg Terminal, deep ocean, starlight on steel |
| **Sage** | Muted green-grey, forest | #5f8c5a | Japanese garden, morning fog, matcha ceramics |
| **Clay** | Terra cotta, dusty rose | #c47a5a | Southwest pottery, desert sunset, adobe walls |
| **Frost** | Cool whites, violet-blue | #7c6aef | Scandinavian winter, frosted glass, aurora borealis |

Each theme defines: parchment, warm50-400, ink900-300, accent/accentLight/accentBg, success, error, info, warning.
