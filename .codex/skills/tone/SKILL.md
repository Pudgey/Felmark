# Tone — Microcopy & Brand Voice Audit

Run a microcopy and brand voice audit on the specified target.

## Sweep for:
- Generic copy ("Something went wrong", "Error", "Success")
- Inconsistent voice (formal in one place, casual in another)
- Missing microcopy (unlabeled icons, unexplained states)
- Placeholder text left in production
- Jargon or technical language in user-facing text
